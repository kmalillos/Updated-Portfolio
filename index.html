About

Have you ever heard of an Operations manager and a wish fulfillment specialist? That's Kaylah. Oh, and she's learning full stack web development.

Kaylah excels at prioritizing and coordinating simultaneous tasks and projects and she thrives in a fast-paced startup environment individually and on a team.

For fun, Kaylah enjoys binging on some Netflix shows, enjoying the Colorado sunshine, and planning her next adventure.
